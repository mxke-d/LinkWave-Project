CHU Sainte-Justine

DAS maintenance and monitoring project for Centre Hospitalier Universitaire Sainte-Justine in Montreal, QC. The deployment was based on the CommScope ION-B platform with Node-A public safety off-air repeaters, supporting UHF LMR, multi-tone and 900 MHz paging, and the SERAM 700/800 MHz public safety network.
