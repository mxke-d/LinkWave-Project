Healthcare

Vaudreuil-Soulange

Neutral-host cellular and LMR+Paging turnkey DAS solution. Tri-carrier capable system for Rogers, Telus, Bell, and Videotron, with UHF LMR and 900MHz paging services.

Transportation

Union Station

Public safety turnkey solution with P25 and conventional radio systems. Supporting Toronto Police, Fire, and EMS services with hybrid fiber/coax infrastructure.

Government

MacDonald Block

Public safety turnkey solution with next-generation fiber to the edge DAS platform. Multi-carrier capable system for Bell, Telus, Rogers, and Freedom, supporting 5G, P25, and 2-way radio for Toronto Police, Fire, and EMS.

Commercial

Brentwood Tower

Public safety E-Comm 911 DAS for residential tower in Burnaby, BC. Passive DAS system with 700/800 MHz P25 Phase 2 technology, covering below and above grade areas with off-air signal source.

Data Centres

Microsoft Data Centres

Turnkey cellular and LMR (PTT) DAS solutions for Class A datacenters. Integrated system supporting LTE, 5G, and MIMO cellular with UHF trunked DMR LMR network, including RF survey, design, and commissioning services.
