WHO ARE WE?

Why Choose
Linkwave?

Linkwave Wireless Solutions delivers high-performance engineering and integration for complex wireless environments. We bridge the gap between rigorous carrier requirements and enterprise needs through precision design and technical excellence, providing infrastructure that is reliable, scalable, and engineered to the highest industry standards.

Vendor-Independent Technical Solutions

We prioritize performance over manufacturer preferences. Our engineers select technology based strictly on technical specifications and your facility's unique RF requirements, ensuring an objective, high-tier solution optimized for your budget and performance goals.

Proven Carrier-Grade Expertise

With over a decade of experience in DAS and indoor wireless network development, our team operates at the forefront of the industry. Our track record of developing specialized engineering tools demonstrates the technical proficiency that ensures your project meets or exceeds carrier standards.

End-to-End Project Management

We provide a single point of responsibility for the entire system lifecycle. From initial site surveys and engineering approvals to deployment and long-term maintenance, we take full ownership of the systems we design, ensuring reliable and future-proof installations.
