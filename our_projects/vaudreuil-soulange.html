CASE STUDY

HÔPITAL VAUDREUIL-SOULANGES

Connecting the Future of Healthcare

Vaudreuil, QC | Industry: Healthcare

Hôpital Vaudreuil-Soulanges represents a monumental leap in Québec's healthcare landscape, serving as a "hospital of the future" that harmonizes digital innovation with human-centric care.

To support this vision, Linkwave deployed an Active DAS to deliver seamless LTE and 5G connectivity for Bell, Telus, Rogers, and Videotron. This robust infrastructure ensures that critical medical workflows and real-time patient data transmission remain uninterrupted across the entire facility.

Beyond Commercial Cellular, Linkwave deployed a Public Safety Indoor DAS to ensure maximum resilience for first responders and hospital staff. Supporting Public Safety Radio, internal operations, and paging, the system provides guaranteed communications across the entire facility.

  • The interior space of Hôpital Vaudreuil-Soulanges will be equivalent to more than 18 professional football fields.
