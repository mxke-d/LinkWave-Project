CASE STUDY

LAKERIDGE GARDENS LONG TERM CARE

Keeping Caregivers and Residents Connected

Ajax, ON | Industry: Healthcare

Linkwave partnered with Lakeridge Health to deliver a comprehensive wireless solution for Lakeridge Gardens, a modern long-term care facility in Ajax, Ontario. Linkwave deployed a robust Active DAS. This infrastructure ensures seamless, high capacity cellular coverage throughout the entire building, eliminating dead zones and providing staff and residents with a stable foundation for critical care applications.

By integrating multi-carrier support for Bell, Telus, and Rogers, the solution provides universal connectivity across both LTE and 5G spectrums. This future-proof architecture allows healthcare providers to utilize high-speed data for real-time monitoring while ensuring residents stay connected with their loved ones through reliable voice and video calls.
