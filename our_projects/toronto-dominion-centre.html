Project Overview

As one of the largest in‑building DAS deployments in Canada, the Toronto Dominion Centre program placed stringent demands on design quality, installation oversight, testing rigor, and delivery timelines. Under Bell Mobility, Linkwave supported four towers through design collaboration, project management, and comprehensive DAS testing and commissioning—meeting tight deadlines and exacting performance requirements.

Key Highlights

  • Multi‑carrier active platform with 11 sectors deployed on Day 1
  • Design prepared for future expansion while maintaining PIM “friendliness”
  • Compliance with evolving tri‑carrier design guidelines
  • Automated DAS testing tools reduced engineering effort by orders of magnitude
  • First‑pass acceptance of network performance across all 4 towers

Design

The system is a multi‑carrier active DAS designed for immediate scale—11 sectors at Day 1— with capacity to expand as demand grows. The design emphasized PIM‑resilient architecture to enable thorough post‑installation testing. A major challenge was aligning with new tri‑carrier guidelines that were being finalized late in the design phase; the project was among the first to be audited to these standards and emerging test requirements.

Project Management

Linkwave managed planning and prioritization, implemented design updates based on construction and landlord feedback, and oversaw installation progress and compliance. Collaboration with Bell and Rogers Wireless engineering teams ensured alignment from rough‑in to final integration.

Testing

Under contract to Bell Mobility, Linkwave developed automated DAS testing tools that transformed the new test workflow—reducing time to generate reports and evaluate field results by orders of tens. The resulting toolset became a national standard for DAS testing at Bell. Linkwave was responsible for evaluating and approving test results for all four TD towers.

Commissioning and Optimization

Commissioning met the carrier’s highest bar. Working alongside Bell engineers, optimizers, and technicians, Linkwave validated KPIs and performance, achieving first‑pass acceptance across all towers.
