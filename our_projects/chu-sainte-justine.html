CASE STUDY

CHU SAINTE-JUSTINE

Precision Coverage for Pediatric Excellence

Montreal, QC | Industry: Healthcare

Linkwave deployed a high-performance Hybrid Fiber/Coax DAS solution to overcome signal blocking in Canada's largest mother-child center. This system eliminates dead zones by providing enterprise grade LTE and 5G connectivity for Rogers, Telus, Bell, and Videotron, ensuring reliable signal for medical staff.

Linkwave's design prioritizes mission-critical reliability by incorporating dedicated infrastructure for Montreal Public Safety radio networks. This ensures first responders maintain crystal-clear communication in shielded areas, meeting safety codes.
