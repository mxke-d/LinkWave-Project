DAS Systems & Wireless Coverage

A Distributed Antenna System (DAS) is a network of antennas that distributes wireless signals throughout buildings to improve cellular coverage. DAS systems solve poor cell phone signal problems in hospitals, stadiums, office buildings, and underground facilities by amplifying and distributing carrier signals evenly across all areas, ensuring reliable wireless connectivity for voice calls, data, and emergency communications.

DAS installation costs vary based on building size, coverage requirements, and system complexity. For large buildings, costs typically range from $2-8 per square foot. We provide detailed DAS cost estimates after conducting comprehensive site surveys and coverage analysis. Our solutions are designed to maximize ROI while ensuring optimal wireless performance throughout your facility.

Active DAS uses fiber optic cables and remote radio units to distribute signals, offering superior performance for large buildings over 500,000 sq ft. Passive DAS uses coaxial cables and passive components, making it more cost-effective for smaller buildings. Active DAS provides better signal quality, easier maintenance, and future-proof scalability, while passive DAS offers lower upfront costs for smaller applications.

Yes, our DAS systems support 5G networks and work with all major Canadian wireless carriers including Bell, Rogers, Telus, and regional providers. We design future-proof DAS solutions that support current 4G LTE and emerging 5G technologies, ensuring your building stays connected as wireless technology evolves. Multi-carrier DAS systems provide comprehensive coverage for all users regardless of their service provider.

DAS installation timelines for hospitals and large facilities typically range from 4-12 weeks, depending on building complexity and coverage requirements. Our process includes site surveys, carrier coordination, design engineering, installation, testing, and commissioning. We work around your operations schedule to minimize disruption and ensure the DAS system meets all healthcare and safety requirements.

Public Safety & Emergency Communications

Public safety DAS (Distributed Antenna System) is required by building codes to ensure emergency responders can communicate reliably inside buildings. Our public safety DAS systems support fire, police, and emergency medical services communications on dedicated frequencies. We ensure compliance with NFPA 72, IBC, and local fire codes while providing redundant systems and backup power for critical emergency communications.

We ensure emergency communication systems meet all code requirements through Professional Engineer (P.Eng) certification, compliance with NFPA 72, IBC, and local fire codes, and comprehensive testing and certification. Our systems include redundant power supplies, backup systems, and regular maintenance to ensure reliable emergency communications when needed most.

We provide comprehensive wireless solutions for first responders including public safety DAS, emergency communication systems, and critical infrastructure wireless networks. We work with emergency services to ensure seamless communication during critical situations.
