Engineering Solutions

Comprehensive Wireless Consulting Services

Expert consulting services tailored to your wireless infrastructure needs, from initial feasibility through implementation support.

Feasibility Studies & Site Analysis

Comprehensive technical and financial analysis to determine the viability of wireless infrastructure projects. We evaluate site conditions, RF propagation, coverage requirements, budget constraints, and technology options to provide data-driven recommendations.

Network Design Review & Validation

Independent third-party review and validation of wireless network designs. Our Professional Engineers assess RF coverage predictions, interference analysis, capacity planning, equipment selection, and installation specifications to ensure code compliance and optimal performance.

RFP & RFQ Development

Expert preparation of Request for Proposal (RFP) and Request for Quotation (RFQ) documents that clearly define project scope, technical specifications, performance requirements, and objective evaluation criteria. We help you attract qualified vendors and compare proposals effectively.

Vendor Selection

Objective evaluation of vendor proposals based on technical capabilities, experience, pricing, and project fit. Our engineers provide unbiased recommendations to support your procurement decisions.

Regulatory Compliance

Guidance on building codes, fire safety requirements, and industry standards including NFPA, OFC, and local Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) regulations for DAS and wireless systems.

Project Management Oversight

Independent oversight and quality assurance throughout project execution. We monitor progress, review deliverables, and verify compliance with approved designs and industry standards.

The Professional Engineering Advantage

Working with licensed Professional Engineers provides critical advantages for wireless infrastructure projects. Our P.Eng. designation represents rigorous education, examination, and ongoing professional development in electrical engineering.

Professional Accountability

Licensed engineers are bound by code of ethics and professional standards, ensuring responsible practice and accountability for technical work.

Regulatory Compliance

Professional Engineers understand building codes, safety regulations, and jurisdictional requirements for wireless infrastructure installations.

Technical Excellence

Engineering expertise ensures designs are technically sound, properly documented, and optimized for performance and cost-effectiveness.

Stamped Drawings

Professional Engineers can stamp and seal technical drawings, often required for building permits and authority approvals.
