Our Partners

We're proud to work alongside industry leaders. These valued partners help us deliver smarter wireless infrastructure, every time.

CORNING

Corning Inc. is a global leader in glass science, ceramics, and optical physics. With over 160 years of innovation, Corning creates materials used in display tech, life sciences, and wireless infrastructure around the world.

COMMSCOPE

CommScope enables faster, smarter, and more reliable networks. From wired to wireless solutions, they help service providers and enterprises boost performance, expand capacity, and modernize network infrastructure.

ICOMERA

Icomera delivers high-speed mobile internet for public transit. Powering WiFi for millions of passengers on 30,000+ buses, trains, and trams, their tech improves safety, efficiency, and digital experience on the move.

SUNWAVE

Sunwave makes wireless coverage tools and network hardware used across Canada. They provide DAS, IoT, and other smart systems to help carriers improve indoor signal, boost speed, and extend range in tough places.

BTI WIRELESS

BTI Wireless designs RF and power amplifier solutions that optimize network reach and quality. Trusted by 14+ global carriers, BTI helps operators enhance signal coverage and boost performance in high-traffic zones.

SOLiD

SOLiD builds tools for better indoor wireless. Their DAS systems are used in places like malls, stadiums, and offices. These tools help carry phone and data signals where normal coverage may not reach or stay strong.

NOVOTECH

Novotech is a top distributor of IoT and M2M tech, offering modems, antennas, and modules. With key partnerships across North America, they enable scalable hardware solutions for automation, tracking, and connectivity.

NOKIA

Nokia makes wireless systems and phones used around the world. They lead the way in 5G tech, helping companies and users stay connected through better, faster networks built for today and ready for what's next.
